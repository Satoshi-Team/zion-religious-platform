## Introduction

Buddhism, founded by Siddhartha Gautama, provides a path to enlightenment through wisdom and compassion.

## Key Concepts

The Four Noble Truths and Eightfold Path form the foundation of Buddhist teachings.

## Historical Context

Originating in ancient India, Buddhism spread across Asia and the world.

## Practical Applications

Mindfulness and meditation practices are widely adopted in modern life.

## Conclusion

Buddhism's universal principles continue to guide seekers of peace and wisdom.
